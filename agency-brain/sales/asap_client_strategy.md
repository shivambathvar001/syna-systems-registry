# ASAP Client Strategy: The 14-Day Sprint

## Goal: Secure one paying "Velocity Pilot" client in 2 weeks.

### Phase 1: The Showcase Asset (Days 1-3)
**Action:** Build a "Killer Demo" relevant to your target niche.
- **Example:** An AI Lead Research Agent that scrapes a LinkedIn profile and writes a hyper-personalized, non-spammy outreach email based on their latest post.
- **Why:** Prospects don't want to hear what you *can* do; they want to see what you *have done*.

### Phase 2: Targeted Prospecting (Days 4-7)
**Action:** Identify 20 "High-Pain" prospects.
- **Target:** B2B SaaS or Agencies with 10-50 employees (likely struggling with manual follow-ups).
- **Tools:** Use `AiAA/scripts/lead_gen.py` to find them.
- **Selection Criteria:** Look for companies hiring SDRs or Support staff (a signal of manual scaling pain).

### Phase 3: The "Value-First" Outreach (Days 8-10)
**Action:** Send the "Loom + Gift" offer.
- **The Pitch:** "I built an AI agent that researched your company and identified [Specific Inefficiency]. I made a 2-minute video showing how I'd automate it. No charge for the video, just wanted to show you what's possible."
- **Follow-up:** Call/Email 24 hours later to book a "Strategic Audit."

### Phase 4: The Pilot Close (Days 11-14)
**Action:** Pitch the "Velocity Pilot."
- **Offer:** "We build this exact workflow for you in 2 weeks for $2,500. If we don't save you 10 hours a week, you don't pay."
- **Risk Reversal:** The "Pay-on-Performance" or "Money-Back" guarantee is essential for the first client.

## Immediate Next Step
1. **Choose Niche:** B2B SaaS vs. Real Estate vs. Agencies.
2. **Build Showcase:** Let's build the Lead Research Agent now.
