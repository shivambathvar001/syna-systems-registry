### [PROJECT BIO-GUARD]
**Sector:** Healthcare Revenue Cycle Management (RCM)
**Challenge:** A multi-state hospital system was losing $14M annually to "Medical Necessity" denials due to clinical documentation lag.
**Declassified Architecture:**
- **The Sanity Gateway:** Implemented a Zero-Trust PII masking layer that redacts sensitive patient data before it reaches the orchestration layer.
- **Stateful Clinical Auditor:** A LangGraph-based workflow that cross-references physician notes against 200+ Payer-specific Local Coverage Determinations (LCD).
- **Predictive Routing:** Integrated a high-precision risk engine that identifies "denial-prone" claims with 92% accuracy, routing them for senior human correction before submission.
**Result:** 12% increase in first-pass clean claims; $8.4M in recovered revenue within 6 months.

### [PROJECT IRON-LOGIC]
**Sector:** International Freight & Logistics
**Challenge:** A global logistics firm faced a 4.2-hour latency in carrier invoice reconciliation, leading to massive billing discrepancies.
**Declassified Architecture:**
- **Supervisor-Worker Mesh:** Deployed a hierarchical multi-agent system where a "Supervisor Agent" governs specialized "Extractor Agents" to ensure 99.9% data accuracy.
- **Self-Healing Resolution:** The system autonomously flags discrepancies and initiates secure MCP (Model Context Protocol) bridges to carrier portals for real-time verification.
- **Immutable Audit Ledger:** Every agentic decision was logged into a forensic-grade ledger for SOC2 compliance.
**Result:** Latency reduced from 4.2 hours to 4 minutes; 90% reduction in billing errors.

### [PROJECT ZERO-BRIDGE]
**Sector:** Enterprise Data Governance (Financial Services)
**Challenge:** A financial services MNC needed to bridge LLMs to proprietary market data without exposing raw database credentials or PII.
**Declassified Architecture:**
- **The Zero-Trust Bridge:** Custom MCP server acting as a "Secure Proxy" between the LLM and the Internal Data Lake.
- **Operational Metric Shield:** The server only exposes aggregated, non-sensitive metrics required for the AI's reasoning, effectively "air-gapping" the core data.
- **Permissioned Tool-Calling:** Restricted agent capabilities to a "White-List" of read-only operations.
**Result:** Enabled autonomous market analysis for the investment team while maintaining 100% data sovereignty.
