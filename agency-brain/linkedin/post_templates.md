## TEMPLATE PHILOSOPHY
Every post follows the same core structure:
1. **HOOK** — Stop the scroll. One punchy line. Stat, provocation, or unexpected fact.
2. **BODY** — Deliver value. Build credibility. Use white space liberally (LinkedIn penalizes walls of text).
3. **CTA** — Drive action. Comment, DM, follow, or think. Never passive.

Rule: **First line = everything.** LinkedIn shows 2-3 lines before "see more." Make them count.

## POST FORMATTING RULES (LinkedIn-Specific)

| Rule | Why |
|------|-----|
| First line must be <220 characters (hook only) | LinkedIn's "see more" cutoff |
| Use line breaks generously — never 3+ consecutive lines | Readability in feed |
| Bold/arrows (→) for lists — no bullet points | Better rendering on mobile |
| Max 5 hashtags | Algorithm penalizes hashtag spam |
| End with a direct question or CTA | Drives comments = reach |
| No links in the body of the post | LinkedIn suppresses outbound links |
| Links in first comment (if needed) | Standard workaround |
| Ideal post length: 900-1,200 characters | Sweet spot for engagement |
