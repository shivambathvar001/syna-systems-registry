# AiAA Platform Architecture & Brand Specs

To project the image of a global AI Automation giant, the digital platform must be technically flawless, content-rich, and authority-driven.

## 1. Domain & SEO Strategy
- **Core Domain:** `aiaa.ai` or `aiaa.io` (suggested).
- **AI-SEO:** Optimize for "Agentic Workflow Orchestration," "Enterprise AI Security," and "Autonomous Operations."
- **Robots.txt & Sitemaps:** Configured to prioritize crawling of high-value solution pages and case studies.

## 2. Platform Sitemap (Enterprise Standard)
- **Home:** High-impact hero, ROI metrics, "Trusted by" (placeholders), and Architecture overview.
- **Solutions (Service Pages):**
    - `solutions/agentic-workflows`: Stateful multi-agent systems.
    - `solutions/intelligent-cx`: AI-native customer experience.
    - `solutions/enterprise-sales`: Autonomous lead-to-close engines.
- **Security & Trust Center:** Crucial for MNCs. Details on SOC2, GDPR, Data Sovereignty, and Zero-Trust AI.
- **Insights (The Content Engine):** High-frequency, technical blog posts and whitepapers.
- **Case Studies / Demos:** Video walkthroughs of "Agentic Architecture" (Mermaid diagrams + UI demos).
- **Company:** About us, Careers (even if placeholders), and Ethics/Governance.

## 3. The Digital Footprint (Social Handles)
- **LinkedIn:** Primary authority channel. Focused on "Build-in-Public" for CEOs/CTOs.
- **X (Twitter):** Technical ecosystem updates and networking with AI researchers.
- **YouTube/Vimeo:** Professional high-def demos of complex workflows.

## 4. Content Engine (AI-SEO Riched)
- **Frequency:** 2 Technical Deep-Dives per week + 1 Strategic Whitepaper per month.
- **Tone:** Professional, objective, engineering-focused.

## 5. Technical Requirements
- **Framework:** Next.js (SEO-optimized, fast, modern).
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS with a custom "Enterprise Dark" theme.
- **Analytics:** Plausible or Vercel Analytics (Privacy-first for enterprises).
