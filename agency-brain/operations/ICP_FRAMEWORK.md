## Target Segment 2: Healthcare Revenue Cycle Management (RCM)
(Specialized Vertical: Denial Management)

### 1. The Core Signal: "Denial Attrition"
We target Healthcare Groups and Hospitals (50-500+ staff) experiencing high first-pass denial rates:
- **RCM Specialists/Analysts:** Hiring for roles focused on "Denial Appeals" or "Billing Recovery."
- **EHR Transitions:** Recent migration to Epic, Cerner, or Athenahealth (high risk of process friction).
- **Consolidation:** Large healthcare groups acquiring smaller practices (need for unified billing infrastructure).

### 2. Industry-Specific Pain Points
- **Medical Necessity Denials:** Missing clinical documentation to support billing codes.
- **Payer Rule Lag:** 30-90 day delays in updating billing rules to match payer policy shifts.
- **Manual Appeals:** Senior staff spent 40+ hours/week writing custom appeal letters.
