# HIPAA Business Associate Agreement (BAA)
**Airwalk AI & [Client Name]**

This Business Associate Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the Effective Date by and between **Airwalk AI LLC** ("Business Associate") and **[Client Name]** ("Covered Entity").

---

## 1. Purpose and Scope
Covered Entity wishes to engage the services of Business Associate to deploy the Post-Denial Resolution Engine (the "Services"). In providing these Services, Business Associate may receive, maintain, create, or transmit Protected Health Information ("PHI") that is subject to protection under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 ("HIPAA") and the HITECH Act.

## 2. Obligations of Business Associate
Business Associate agrees to:
* **Permitted Uses and Disclosures:** Only use or disclose PHI as permitted or required by this Agreement, the Master Services Agreement (MSA), or as required by law.
* **Safeguards:** Implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards that reasonably and appropriately protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of Electronic PHI (ePHI) in compliance with the HIPAA Security Rule.
* **PII/PHI Scrubbing:** Route all EHR data payloads through the local zero-trust proxy gateway to filter and strip patient identifiers prior to any processing outside the local network.
* **Breach Notification:** Report to Covered Entity any acquisition, access, use, or disclosure of PHI not provided for by this Agreement (a "Breach") within seventy-two (72) hours of discovery.
* **Subcontractors:** Ensure that any subcontractors or agents that create, receive, maintain, or transmit PHI on behalf of Business Associate agree to the same restrictions and conditions.
* **Access to Books and Records:** Make internal practices, books, and records relating to the use and disclosure of PHI available to the Secretary of HHS for purposes of determining compliance.

## 3. Term and Termination
* **Term:** This Agreement shall remain in effect until terminated or until all PHI provided by Covered Entity to Business Associate is destroyed or returned.
* **Termination for Cause:** Upon Covered Entity's knowledge of a material breach by Business Associate, Covered Entity shall provide an opportunity to cure the breach. If cure is not possible or fails within thirty (30) days, Covered Entity may immediately terminate the MSA.
* **Effect of Termination:** Upon termination, Business Associate shall return or destroy all PHI received from Covered Entity. If return or destruction is infeasible, Business Associate shall extend the protections of this Agreement to such PHI and limit further uses and disclosures.
