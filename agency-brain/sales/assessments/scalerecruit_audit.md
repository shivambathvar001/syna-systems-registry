# Syna Systems | Friction-to-ROI Assessment: ScaleRecruit

## Executive Summary
**Observation:** ScaleRecruit is hiring an Operations Coordinator to manage candidate intake.
**Problem:** Manual triage creates a "Candidate Latency Gap," where top talent is lost to faster-moving competitors.

## Proposed Architecture: Talent Research Agent
1. **Signal Trigger:** New candidate application in Greenhouse/Lever.
2. **Autonomous Research:** Agent enriches profile via GitHub, LinkedIn, and Technical Blogs.
3. **Audit Node:** Agent generates a "Technical Suitability Report" (90% accuracy vs human screening).
4. **HITL Route:** Recruiter receives a prioritized, enriched dashboard for final interview selection.

## Projections
- **Time Savings:** 12 hrs/week per recruiter.
- **ROI:** 4.2x efficiency gain within 30 days.
- **Status:** READY FOR PILOT.
