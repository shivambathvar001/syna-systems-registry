# Healthcare Revenue Cycle Management (RCM)
## Predictive Denial Management & Clinical Documentation Integrity

### The Challenge: The $10M Leak
For enterprise healthcare systems, claim denials are not just a nuisance; they are a direct hit to the bottom line. With an industry-average denial rate of 10-15%, millions of dollars in earned revenue are lost to preventable documentation gaps and complex payer rules.

### The Syna Solution: Operational AI Infrastructure
Syna Systems moves beyond simple "billing software" to deliver an **Agentic Infrastructure** that eliminates denials before they occur. Our **Predictive Denial Management (PDM) Scrubber** integrates directly with your EHR to provide real-time, stateful oversight of every claim.

#### 1. Clinical Documentation Auditor (CDA)
Our CDA agents analyze physician notes in real-time, cross-referencing documentation against Local Coverage Determinations (LCD).
- **Impact:** 40% reduction in medical necessity denials.
- **Outcome:** Clean claims that are defensible from day one.

#### 2. Dynamic Payer Rules Engine
The Payer Rules Engine is a self-evolving knowledge base that ingests policy updates from thousands of payers, eliminating the "manual update lag."
- **Impact:** 98%+ Clean Claim Rate (CCR).
- **Outcome:** Total alignment with ever-shifting payer requirements.

#### 3. Autonomous Risk Routing
Not all claims require the same level of human intervention. Our system assigns a **Denial Risk Score** to every transaction, routing low-risk claims for automatic submission and flagging high-risk cases for senior human review.
- **Impact:** 300% ROI on labor optimization.
- **Outcome:** Human expertise focused on the most complex cases.
