## ENGAGEMENT TACTICS

### Comment-First Strategy
**Rule:** Post your content → immediately go to 3-5 posts by target audience and leave substantive comments.

Why it works: LinkedIn boosts your visibility to the commenter's network when they see your activity. This seeds your post with early impressions from decision-makers.

**Comment framework:**
```
[Acknowledge their insight] + [Add your own perspective] + [Bridge to your expertise]

Example:
"This is exactly what we're seeing with Epic's new features — the documentation 
quality improves but the payer policy alignment gap is still there. 
The problem is upstream of where most HIT tools operate."
```

**How many:** 3-5 comments BEFORE your own post goes up. Then 3-5 more within 60 min after posting.

### Creator Mode Tips
1. **Enable Creator Mode** — shows "Follow" instead of "Connect," grows audience faster
2. **Set your topics:** Select `Healthcare`, `Revenue Cycle`, `Artificial Intelligence` as creator topics
3. **Add newsletter:** Consider "Syna Authority Digest" weekly newsletter — repurposes post content to email subscribers
4. **Featured section:** Pin your best-performing case study post to profile top
5. **Post consistently** — 3x/week for 4+ weeks triggers LinkedIn's creator algorithm boost
